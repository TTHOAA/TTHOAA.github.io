Los Van Van

Bio

Los Van Van is Cuba's most popular post-revolution music group. The band "Los Van Van" was established in 1969 by the late Juan Formell. Juan Formell's vision of incorporating North American influences into Cuban music set the wheels in motion for Cuban music to once again continue to develop new rhythms after a period of stagnation. Los Van Van is perhaps most known for being the main developers of what is now called Timba, the youngest genre of Cuban music influenced by salsa, jazz, funk, and its popular and Afrocuban predecessors.
